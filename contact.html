About Myself

" This is me (to the right), Mansi Mangukiya, I am a Senior at NJIT and soon to be graduating with an IT degree in May 2021. I can't describe my excitement upon the completion of one of the biggest milestones in my life. It is quite exciting and volatile for my upcoming journey. I am ready for what the world has to bring forward to me. This new chapter post college will bring in many paths that I have never taken before, it will uncover many sides about myself that I may have not known about me. All I wish for myself is that I am prepared for any challenges that come along my way. My goal in life is to always love what I am doing. There is so much more in the world yet to learn,wish me luck!!"


Why I chose IT? When I was young our router used to break down a lot and when that happened everyone would call me to fix it. If something in the computer or something would not work on the computer everyone in family would call me. ("Mansi to the rescue!") To fix that all I would do is turn off and turn on the computer (literally!) and they would think I did something so amazing. Since that day I realized, if a machine is fixed from the most simplest actions what about the machines that are operated in companies like Google,Apple, Microsoft, Amazon and many more billion dollar companies. From that small experience I decided that I wanted to go and do something in the IT field, I wasn't sure back then what exactly I wanted to do but I knew I was definitely going in the IT field. One fine day it is my dream to work for these amazing billion dollar companies.
